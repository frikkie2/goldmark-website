Our Story

Goldmark Real Estate was started in 2000 by Johan and Ina van Niekerk. It is a niche, family-owned business with specialization in Pretoria East.

The partners strategically choose to keep the business small and specialized as this allows Goldmark Real Estate to supply exceptional service with a personal touch.

"The Agent That Walks The Extra Mile" — this isn't just our slogan, it's our promise to every client.

Whether you want to sell or buy, Goldmark Real Estate brings over 25 years of local market knowledge, dedication, and personal attention to every transaction. We believe that real estate is about people, not just properties.
